Strategic BNB Reserve For Token Buybacks

The CS Strategic Reserve

A BNB locker, built into the Coin Sack smart contract and used exclusively for token buybacks and dead token reinjections.

8% of each token purchase, and 10% of each token sale, is taken in fees and automatically liquidated into BNB to be stored within the strategic reserve by the Coin Sack smart contract. This strategic reserve can only be accessed to perform token buybacks from the Pancake Pool or to reinject dead tokens back into the market as pool liquidity. This has the end effect of increasing or stabalizing the token's value and protects holders from downward market trends.

The Coin Sack smart contract is capable of performing automatic token buybacks using strategic reserve funds. When the reserve exceeds 1 BNB, 2% of the reserve is used during each transaction to buyback CS tokens from the Pancake Pool.


Token Fees

The Coin Sack token uses a proven and battle tested fees structure which is designed to protect holders' investments, and the token's value, while furthering the efforts of the Coin Sack project as a whole.


Refi Static Holder Distribution

Coin Sack holders are natrually rewarded through a refi distribution fee of 4% on PancakeSwap buys and 5% on PancakeSwap sells.


Strategic Reserve Tax

The strategic reserve tax fees, 8% on PancakeSwap buys and 10% on PancakeSwap sells, are automatically liquidated into BNB which is then locked in the smart contract's reserve for buybacks.


Management Contribution

A management contribution fee of 3% on PancakeSwap buys and 5% on PancakeSwap sells is automatically liquidated into BNB. This BNB is distributed to the Coin Sack management team to further the project.


Coin Sack token fees total 15% for PancakeSwap buys and 20% for PancakeSwap sells. No fees are applied when tokens are transfered between other addresses.
